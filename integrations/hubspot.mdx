## Supported CRMs

| CRM | Status |
|-----|--------|
| **HubSpot** | Available |
| Salesforce | Coming soon |

## What gets synced

When you connect your CRM, OPS syncs three entity types:

| Entity | Data synced |
|--------|------------|
| **Deals** | Name, stage, pipeline, amount, close date, owner, custom fields |
| **Companies** | Name, domain, lifecycle stage, owner, custom fields |
| **Contacts** | Email, name, lifecycle stage, owner, custom fields |

OPS associates CRM records with calls by matching participant email addresses.

## Connecting your CRM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Org Settings">
    Navigate to **Organization Settings** → **Integrations**. You'll need Org Admin permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect HubSpot">
    Click **Connect** on the HubSpot card. An OAuth popup will appear — authorize OPS to access your HubSpot account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure sync settings">
    After connecting, click **Settings** to configure:
    - **Sync Settings** — toggle Deals, Companies, and Contacts on or off
    - **Field Settings** — select which fields to sync (up to 10 custom fields per entity)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sync schedule

- CRM data syncs automatically **every 4 hours**
- You can trigger a manual resync from the integration settings (rate-limited to once per hour)
- OAuth tokens are refreshed automatically

## How CRM data is used

Once connected, CRM data enhances OPS in several ways:

- **Filtering** — filter slices, posts, and runs by CRM deal, company, stage, or amount
- **Enrichment** — CRM context is included when slices analyze calls, providing richer evidence
- **Custom fields** — map CRM fields to create custom filters (e.g., filter by industry, deal size tier, or any custom property)

## Filter typeaheads

The API provides typeahead endpoints for building filter UIs with CRM data:

- `/filters/crm-deals` — search deals by name
- `/filters/crm-companies` — search companies by name
- `/filters/crm-deal-stages` — list available deal stages
- `/filters/crm-fields` — list configured custom fields and their valid values

<Note>
  CRM reference data (deals, companies, stages, fields) is **org-scoped** because the CRM connection is shared across the organization. Calls and posts remain **team-scoped**.
</Note>

## Disconnecting

You can disconnect your CRM at any time from Org Settings. Disconnecting clears the OAuth tokens but retains previously synced CRM data.
