## Connect Notion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Org Settings">
    Navigate to **Org Settings → Integrations**. You'll need Org Admin permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect on Notion">
    An OAuth popup will open — authorize OnePerfectSlice to access your Notion workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a destination page">
    After connecting, a settings modal opens. Search for and select the Notion page or database where you want content to land.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what to sync">
    Enable the sync types you want:

    | Sync type | What gets pushed to Notion |
    |-----------|---------------------------|
    | **Call Reviews** | Summaries and scorecards for each processed call |
    | **Insight Briefs** | AI-generated insight briefs |
    | **Slice Runs** | Results from completed slice analyses |

    Each toggle can be turned on or off independently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After connecting

- Content syncs automatically when new results are generated
- Each sync type tracks its own "last synced" timestamp
- You can change the destination page or toggle sync types at any time from the Notion settings card

## Managing your connection

From the Notion integration card in Org Settings, you can:

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Edit destination** | Change which Notion page receives synced content |
| **Toggle sync types** | Turn individual sync types on or off |
| **Reconnect** | Re-authorize if your Notion token was revoked |
| **Disconnect** | Remove the connection (existing Notion pages are preserved) |
