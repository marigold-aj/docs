---

## Who it's for

- **Sales & Marketing Operations** — make every team, agent, and workflow customer-aware
- **GTM Engineers and Marketing Engineers** — build context-aware automations and AI workflows without stitching together fragmented data
- **Demand Gen Teams** — improve targeting and campaign performance with timely signals from across your stack
- **Product Marketing Teams** — uncover customer, market, and competitive intelligence to sharpen positioning and messaging
- **Sales Enablement Teams** — coach reps with evidence from real conversations and reinforce winning behaviors at scale

## What you can do

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Feed your AI agents** | Agents get real-time, compiled context from across your stack via MCP. No copy-pasting, no manual context-building. |
| **Power your workflows** | Connect OnePerfectSlice to Zapier, n8n, Make, Clay, or your own code via the public API. |
| **Equip your team** | Summaries, scorecards, and evidence show up where your people work — decisions happen faster, with less back-and-forth. |
