## What are slices?

Slices are pre-built AI analyses that answer specific business questions by examining your call transcripts and CRM data. Each slice gathers evidence, runs it through an LLM, and produces structured, scored output.

OPS includes **27 pre-built slices** organized by business objective:

| Objective | What it covers | Example slices |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **Pipeline** | Grow your pipeline | Campaign ideas, content ideas, messaging hooks |
| **Deals** | Win more deals | Enablement priorities, objection patterns |
| **Expand** | Grow existing accounts | Expansion priorities, stakeholder mapping |
| **Product** | Improve your product | Roadmap priorities, competitive intel |

## How to run a slice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the catalog">
    View available slices in the app, filtered by your role or business objective.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure filters">
    Narrow the analysis scope with filters:
    - **Date range** (up to 90 days lookback)
    - **Call types** (discovery, demo, etc.)
    - **Call owners** (specific reps)
    - **Company domains**
    - **CRM deals, companies, or stages**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview">
    Use "Preview Count" to see how many calls match your filters before committing to a run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run">
    Start the analysis. Runs execute asynchronously — you can leave and come back.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review results">
    When complete, view structured output with a quality score (0.0 to 1.0) assessing completeness, specificity, grounding, and actionability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore evidence">
    Drill into the evidence: see which calls and CRM records supported each insight, with direct quotes and confidence scores.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Run lifecycle

A slice run progresses through these phases:

```
queued → gathering_evidence → analyzing_calls → synthesizing → completed
```

If something goes wrong, the run moves to `failed` with an error message.

## Evidence

Every insight is backed by traceable evidence. Evidence items include:

- **Quote** — the exact text from a transcript or CRM record
- **Source** — which call or CRM object the quote came from
- **Confidence** — a score from 0.0 to 1.0

Evidence is grouped by element (the specific thing being evaluated, like "budget confirmation" or "competitive mention").
