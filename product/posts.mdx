## What are posts?

Posts are the outputs generated when OPS processes a call. Every analyzed call produces one or more posts, depending on your team's template configuration.

## Post types

| Type | What it contains |
|------|-----------------|
| **Summary** | High-level call recap — key topics discussed, decisions made, action items, and next steps |
| **Scorecard** | Detailed performance evaluation with element-by-element scores and coaching (see [Scorecards](/product/scorecards)) |

## How posts are generated

1. A call is ingested into OPS (from your connected recording platform)
2. OPS matches the call to a **call type** (discovery, demo, etc.)
3. The appropriate templates are applied (summary, scorecard, or both)
4. The LLM processes the transcript along with any available CRM context
5. The result is saved as a post

This happens automatically — no manual action required.

## Browsing posts

In the app, you can:
- View a chronological feed of all posts for your team
- **Filter by** call type, template type (summary/scorecard), date range, call owner, or company
- **Search** across post content
- Click into any post to see the full result with metadata

## Post metadata

Each post includes:
- **Title** — the call name (may include an AI-generated title)
- **Call owner** — who ran the call
- **Company domain** — the external participant's company
- **Template type** — summary or scorecard
- **Timestamps** — when the call happened and when the post was created

## Accessing posts via the API

The API provides three post endpoints:

- **Search posts** — filter and paginate across your team's posts
- **Get post** — retrieve a single post with full detail
- **List filters** — discover available filter values

Use `detail: true` in search requests to get full post payloads, or `detail: false` (default) for compact summaries.

See the [API Reference](/api-reference) for full documentation.
